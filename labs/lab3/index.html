Chicken Alferdo

My Favorite dish is chicken alfredo, a comfort food that my friend and I always made after highschool.

As for a special memory, I just really enjoy this food as it reminds me of how life was back then.

Additionly, The dish originated in Rome in the early 20th century with Alfredo Di Lelio. The original

recipe was simple, made with butter, Parmesan cheese, and pasta water.

2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
2 tablespoons salt
